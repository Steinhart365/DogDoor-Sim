Our First Impression (For Those Who Are New):

ישיבת הקדוש אמת ואמונה is a unique boys מתיבתא located in Lakewood, New Jersey. We never exceed 25 boys in a year, and they are sometimes divided further depending on the group. We take pride in producing real עבדי השם who excel in learning, davening, השקפת התורה , and all facets of Avodas Hashem. Our priority always has been and always will be the development of the precious תלמדים into the greatest men they can possibly be. Our רביים are expert מחנכים and tremendous צדיקים hand-picked from around the globe by our esteemed Rosh Yeshiva, HaRav Yitzchok Yeshayahu Bergfeldsteinman. The most important factor that sets us apart is that we quickly integrate all students and staff alike into one family, who want only the very best for one another and push each other to succeed. With that said, we would be so honored to get to know you. ברוך הבא!!
