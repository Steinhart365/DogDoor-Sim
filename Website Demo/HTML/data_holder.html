1. Davening is at dawn, and usually takes about 60 minutes
  2. Morning Seder is from 60 minutes after davening until 11:50
  3. Shiur starts ar 12 SHARP, and lasts until around 1 most days
  4. Mincha starts at 2:30
  5. Afternoon Seder including half an hour of Mussar and a ten minute refresher, seder is until 6:30
  6. For secular studies students, their classes are 4:00 to 6:00
  7. The evening seder is 8 to 10:30, followed by Maariv
  8. Immediately after maariv is a twenty minute halacha seder with optional shiur

While we do not encourage higher secular educatin for all, we understand that many will need to move onward and we offer very intense math and science courses during the afternoon, for those who want them. Our students who take the SAT, which we offer a prep class for in 11th grade, average about a 1200, way above the national average.
